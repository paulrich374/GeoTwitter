• Tip #1: Search

    Search and navigate from the same box. Choose from results that appear as you type.

  • Tip #2: Open and scroll tabs

    Open unlimited tabs. Scroll through to find the one you want.

  • Tip #3: Access instantly

    Long press to add Chrome to your dock.

  • Tip #4: Sign in

    Sign in to Chrome to see your open tabs, bookmarks, passwords and omnibox data from your computer and other devices.

  • Tip #5: Incognito

    Browse in private with incognito tabs. Pages you view won't appear in your history or leave traces like cookies.

    Touch the tab switcher icon to switch in or out of incognito mode.
